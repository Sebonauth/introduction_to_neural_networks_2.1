From Biological Inspiration to Artificial Networks

Imagine you're holding a hot cup of coffee, and suddenly it starts to slip. Before you even consciously think about it, your hand has already tightened its grip. How did your brain react so quickly? The answer lies in the incredible network of neurons in your brain – about 86 billion of them – working together in perfect harmony. Today, we're going to explore how these remarkable biological cells inspired one of the most powerful tools in artificial intelligence.
